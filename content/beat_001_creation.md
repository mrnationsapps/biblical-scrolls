## What this proof demonstrates (mechanics → where they're specified)
- **News Outlets carry the Spine** when there are no human posters — the Narrative Guarantee in action (DESIGN §4.7, §5.5). Outlets now established as recurring Pages.
- **Divine-portrayal policy** (§3.1): God acts through events/news, never a profile; the serpent gets no account.
- **Spine vs Texture** tagging (`Narrative_Weight`, §5.5) on every item — the Spine reaches everyone; the texture is optional flavor.
- **Voice-tuning from the character row**: Adam posts in his `Reflective`, emoji-0 voice (§6.3).
- **Canonicity tags**: news reports are `Canonical` (straight from Gen 1, modernized); Adam's interior life and the outlets' banter are `Inferred` (DESIGN §6.4) — *nothing contradicts Scripture.*
- **Post formats** (`POST_FORMATS.md`): news report, daily-life, lifestyle, foreshadow.
- **Reactions** — the wholesome set (Like 👍 / Heart ❤️ / Pray 🙏 / Amen 🕊️ / Sad 😢), and
  crucially **population-bounded**: a post can only be reacted to by who exists in-world at that
  story-moment. Creation is silent; counts swell as the world fills (anti-vanity-metric, on-brand
  with §4.9). *This is the fix for "8.9k hearts when only Adam exists."*
- **Onboarding / trickle (per user feedback)** — a new user lands "already in progress": a
  **scrollable backlog** up to their current point (so they can read in and get oriented), with
  **new posts & comments trickling in live over hours** from there forward — never a one-time
  dump. *(Pairs with a likely move to per-user timelines: every user starts at Genesis.)*
- **Trickle-in & time-aware comments** (§4.6): a reply from a character who didn't exist when the post was made.
- **Ember-watering** (`STORY_THREADS.md`): the stars → Bethlehem; the lone Adam → Eve; the tree → the Fall. The supreme arc starts glowing on Day 4.
- **PYMK** seeded for Eve.
- **Pacing**: 7 biblical days compressed into ~1.3 real-world days per `beats.csv`.
